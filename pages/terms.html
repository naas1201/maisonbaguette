Promotion Terms & Conditions

Click on a category to view its promotions. Then click on a promotion to see its 16 criteria.

Self Promotion
Template Promotion 1
  • Discount Percentage/Amount
    The explicit reduction (e.g., 20% off, $5 off) applied to the product or service.
  • Promotion Duration
    The time frame during which the promotion is valid (e.g., 1 week, weekend-only).
  • Frequency
    How often the promotion is repeated (daily, weekly, seasonal, or one-time).
  • Target Audience
    Specific demographics (students, seniors, loyalty members, new customers).
  • Channel-Specific Offers
    Offers based on where the promotion is advertised (app-exclusive, in-store, online).
  • Bundling/Combos
    Discounts for grouping items together (e.g., meal deals).
  • Volume Discounts
    Reduced rates for bulk purchases (e.g., "Buy 2, get 1 free").
  • Time-Based Pricing
    Dynamic pricing tied to time (happy hour, early-bird specials).
  • Loyalty Program Integration
    Integration with loyalty rewards (e.g., points redeemable for discounts).
  • Geographic Adjustments
    Regional pricing variations based on local demand.
  • Seasonality
    Promotions aligned with holidays or seasonal demand.
  • Payment Condition
    Conditions based on competitor promotions to remain competitive.
  • Minimum Purchase Requirements
    E.g., "Spend 25 to unlock a discount."
  • Terms and Conditions
    Restrictions such as blackout dates or limitations on redeemable items.
  • Creative Presentation
    Marketing style (e.g., "Flash Sale!" vs. "Members-Only Discount") to enhance perceived value.
  • Other if Applicable
    Any additional criteria not covered above.
