Promotion Terms & Conditions

Review the criteria for our different promotions. Click on a category to expand details.

Self Promotion
  • Discount Percentage/Amount
    The explicit reduction (e.g., 20% off, $5 off) applied to the product or service.
  • Promotion Duration
    The time frame during which the promotion is valid (e.g., 1 week, weekend-only, limited-time offers).
  • Frequency
    How often the promotion is repeated (daily, weekly, seasonal, or one-time).
  • Target Audience
    Tailoring rates for specific demographics (students, seniors, loyalty members, new customers).
  • Channel-Specific Offers
    Variations based on where the promotion is advertised (e.g., app-exclusive deals, in-store vs. online).
  • Bundling/Combos
    Discounts for grouping items (e.g., "meal deals" with a burger, fries, and drink).
  • Volume Discounts
    Reduced rates for bulk purchases (e.g., "Buy 2, get 1 free").
  • Time-Based Pricing
    Dynamic pricing tied to time (e.g., happy hour, early-bird specials).
  • Loyalty Program Integration
    Rewards or points redeemable for discounts (e.g., 10% off after 5 purchases).
  • Geographic Adjustments
    Regional pricing variations due to cost of living, competition, or local demand.
  • Seasonality
    Promotions aligned with holidays, festivals, or seasonal demand (e.g., summer BBQ deals).
  • Payment Condition
    Adjustments based on competitorsâ€™ promotions to stay competitive.
  • Minimum Purchase Requirements
    Conditions like "Spend 25 to unlock a discount."
  • Terms and Conditions
    Restrictions (exclusions, blackout dates, or limitations on redeemable items).
  • Creative Presentation
    How the promotion is marketed (e.g., "Flash Sale!" vs. "Members-Only Discount") to enhance perceived value.
  • Other if Applicable
    A flexible field for any additional criteria not covered above.
